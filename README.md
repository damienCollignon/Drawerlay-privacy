# Privacy Policy – Drawerlay

Drawerlay respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
The app does not collect any personally identifiable information.

## Camera and Photo Access
Drawerlay uses the camera and photo library only to allow users to select and display images for drawing. These data remain on your device and are never transmitted.

## Ads
The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect anonymous data to provide relevant ads. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchases
Drawerlay offers a one-time purchase to remove ads. No subscription is used.

## Data Sharing
No personal data is shared with third parties, except for advertising services (Google AdMob).

## Security
All data remain on your device and are not stored or transmitted by the app.
